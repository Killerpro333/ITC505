2 – SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • World Renowned Name: Almost everyone can recognize the name Lamborghini.
  • Technology: Belonging to Volkswagen group, Lamborghini has access to state-of-the-art tech.

Weaknesses

  • High Price: The high price faces the most backlash at times of economic crises.
  • High Fuel Consumption: The powerful cars use loads of fuel, threatening the environment.

Opportunities

  • Expand the Availability: Right now, Lamborghini is out of reach for many people.
  • Increase in Better Customer Service: It’s hard to source parts of a Lamborghini model if something goes wrong.

Threats

  • Competitors: Competitors are rising, producing ultra-premium cars.
  • Government policies: Government policies against high fuel consuming super cars.

In this styled version of the essay, I made several presentational CSS choices to enhance readability and match the luxury and high-end feel of the Lamborghini brand. The font pair (Montserrat for headings and Merriweather for body) offers a modern, sophisticated look.

  • Font Choices: I chose Montserrat for its bold, clean appearance for headings, giving the essay a modern and professional look, while Merriweather provides readability in the body.
  • Text Size: The hierarchy in text sizes makes navigation through the content seamless. The largest size was reserved for the title (36px), with gradually smaller sizes for sections (28px) and subsections (24px).
  • Color Choices: I opted for neutral background colors to ensure that the text stands out. Lamborghini's signature yellow (#FFD700) was used for emphasis, especially on key terms like "Lamborghini" or "SWOT".
  • Spacing: Consistent margins and padding ensure the content is easy to read, with ample white space around text blocks to prevent clutter.
