Adventure Awaits

You wake up in a strange room. What do you do?

Addendum

Process and Planning: I started by outlining the story, which involves a mysterious forest and various decision points leading to multiple endings. Planning the flowchart helped visualize each decision path and track the different outcomes.

Creative Choices: For visuals, I chose a cohesive fantasy theme with forest, river, dragon, and treasure images. The colors and fonts were selected to create an immersive experience that aligns with the story.

Challenges and Solutions: Maintaining smooth transitions and organizing the story choices required careful debugging. The reset button allows the player to restart without refreshing the page, enhancing usability.

Insights: Working on this project provided valuable experience in DOM manipulation and event handling, as well as a deeper understanding of JavaScript's role in interactive web applications.
